Weekly Horoscopes

April 20–26 · Sayari Moon Astrology

Today, Mercury’s contact with Neptune, Saturn, and Mars in Aries creates a demand for heavy lifting, but we’ve somehow lost the plot. Why are we doing this again? This energy is great for creative problem-solving, but with the Moon also Void of Course in Gemini, actions requiring tangible results are better postponed until Wednesday. Midweek, Venus conjuncts Uranus in Taurus for the final time (in our lifetimes), which can bring sudden disruptions to our social and relational dynamics. Then, Venus drifts off into Gemini, lightening the mood by opening space for playful experimentation and mental exploration. The weekend gets intense with a Sun-Pluto square along with Uranus entering Gemini, which it will traverse for the next 7 years. This is the astrological event on everyone’s lips! We’ll see seismic shifts as a collective: our ways of thinking, communicating, and interacting with systems will drastically transform. Everyone plays a key role based on where Gemini is in their chart. By Sunday, the energy softens; the Sun is in Taurus, the Moon is in Virgo, and we have a cute sextile between Venus and Neptune. Sensual and practical matters are emphasized, making it a good time to tend to home, health, and romance. However, with Mercury square Jupiter, beware of over-romanticizing situations and overpromising what you can realistically deliver.
