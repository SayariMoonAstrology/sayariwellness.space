Sayari Moon Astrology

Today, Mercury’s contact with Neptune, Saturn, and Mars in Aries creates a demand for heavy lifting, but we’ve somehow lost the plot. Why are we doing this again? This energy is great for creative problem-solving, but with the Moon also Void of Course in Gemini, actions requiring tangible results are better postponed until Wednesday. Midweek, Venus conjuncts Uranus in Taurus for the final time (in our lifetimes), which can bring sudden disruptions to social and relational dynamics. Then Venus moves into Gemini, lightening the mood and opening space for playful experimentation and mental exploration. The weekend intensifies with a Sun-Pluto square and Uranus entering Gemini for a long transit that will reshape communication, thinking, and collective systems over the next 7 years. By Sunday, the energy softens: the Sun is in Taurus, the Moon is in Virgo, and Venus forms a sextile to Neptune. Sensual and practical matters are emphasized, but with Mercury square Jupiter, avoid overpromising or overidealizing outcomes.

Aries Rising

If there’s something serious you need to say, be mindful of delivery; you can’t take words back. Around Thursday, an unexpected financial opportunity could come through a collaboration. It may feel exciting but unstable. Moving into the weekend, a disagreement around creative control or a friend’s intrusion could test your patience. Don’t force your will. Redirect that energy into something you’re building creatively. You’re entering a longer period of mental and social shifts. Let curiosity lead instead of control.
